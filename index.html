Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you teach?

I teach students of all ages - from young children (5+) to adults. My teaching methodology is adapted to suit each age group's learning style and pace.

Do I need any prior knowledge to start?

No prior knowledge is required! Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, I'll assess your level and create a personalized learning plan for you.

What platform do you use for online classes?

Classes are conducted via Zoom, Google Meet, or Skype - whichever platform you're most comfortable with. All you need is a stable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone.

Can I reschedule or cancel a class?

Yes! I understand life can be unpredictable. You can reschedule classes with at least 24 hours notice. We'll find a suitable alternative time that works for both of us.

How long does it take to learn to read Quran?

The timeline varies for each student depending on their dedication and practice. On average, with consistent effort, most students can read basic Quran within 3-6 months. Mastering Tajweed takes additional time.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes! Standard and Premium plan students receive digital study materials including PDFs, practice sheets, and audio recordings. I also recommend specific books and resources based on your learning goals.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods. Payment is typically made at the beginning of each month.

Can I try a free trial class?

Absolutely! I offer a free 20-minute trial class so you can experience my teaching style and we can discuss your learning goals. Contact me to schedule your trial!
